The Roadmap

Five Phases. Five Months.

1
Phase 1 · Validation
Does anyone actually buy this?
June 1 – June 30, 2026
▾
The Mission Get your first paid sales. Find out your Cost Per Purchase (CPP). Understand if the product can be profitable with paid ads. This is the only question that matters right now — everything else is noise.
Week 1 · June 1–7
  • Confirm pixel is firing correctly on Shopify ✓
  • Launch first Meta ad campaign — 2–3 ad sets, £5–10/day each
  • Test 2–3 different creatives (UGC-feel + clean product shot)
  • Hook to test: "Ditch the Tripod" — gym rack snap, quick setup
Week 2 · June 8–14
  • Read the data: CPM, CTR, Add to Cart, Purchases
  • Kill anything with CTR below 1%
  • Zero purchases at £30–40 spend? New creative angle — not new product
  • Purchases at £15–25 CPP? You have something to work with
Weeks 3–4 · June 15–30
  • Iterate on what's working. Kill what isn't.
  • Test one new audience or creative per week minimum
  • Goal: at least 5–10 total sales by end of June
✓ Success Metric

First sale made. CPP understood. You know which creative direction is working.

⚠ Red Flag

Zero purchases after £100 spend across multiple creatives = the ad angle is wrong, not the product. Change the hook entirely before giving up.

2
Phase 2 · Optimisation
Find the winning formula.
July 1 – July 31, 2026
▾
The Mission You've got data. Now find the ad that prints. One creative + audience combo that produces consistent purchases at a CPP that leaves profit margin. At £12.99, you need CPP below £8–9 to be profitable after product cost and shipping.
Weeks 1–2 · July 1–14
  • Scale budget on winning ad sets (double if CPP is profitable)
  • Kill anything not converted after £20–30 spend
  • New creative: before/after (chaotic gym filming vs. clean Rivit setup)
  • Test video vs. static if you haven't already
Weeks 3–4 · July 15–31
  • Winning ad? Duplicate the ad set, increase budget 20% at a time
  • Start collecting customer emails — you'll need the list later
  • First organic content: one Instagram Reel per week (real gym use)
✓ Success Metric

£300–500 revenue for the month. One ad creative that's consistently profitable.

⚠ Red Flag

Still no winning creative after £200 total spend. Reconsider the creative strategy — you may need UGC from real gym-goers, not polished product shots.

3
Phase 3 · First Real Revenue
Prove it's a real business.
August 1 – August 31, 2026
▾
The Mission Hit £500+ revenue in a single month. Scale what's working. Start building brand infrastructure beyond just ads.
Weeks 1–2 · Aug 1–14
  • Scaling winning ad sets
  • Add second product test if Flex Ring is proven
  • Start post-purchase email sequence (thank you → review → repeat)
  • First organic push: 3 Reels this month targeting gym content creators
Weeks 3–4 · Aug 15–31
  • Review monthly numbers honestly
  • Profitable → plan September budget increase
  • Borderline → cut costs, focus organic, don't scale until margin is fixed
  • Personal brand: start building Dane's face behind the brand
✓ Success Metric

£500+ revenue. Positive ROAS. Email list started.

⚠ Watch Out

People buy from people. Don't be a faceless brand — your personal angle is a real competitive advantage in the gym content space.

4
Phase 4 · Consistency + Systems
Build something that runs without you.
September 1 – October 31, 2026
▾
The Mission This phase matters most for Thailand. If Rivit Co. needs your constant attention to survive, you can't travel with it. You need systems that run in 1 hour/day.
September
  • Automate: email flows, order fulfilment, customer service templates
  • Document your ad process — manageable in 1 hour/day
  • Target: £750+ revenue
  • Organic: 8–10 Reels, building a gym-goer audience
October
  • Stress-test the systems. Could you run this from your phone for a week?
  • Target: £1,000+ revenue
  • Start thinking about next product — what do customers actually want?
  • Pre-Thailand goal: 3 months consistent sales data
✓ Success Metric

Business runs with 1 hour/day. £1k revenue in October. Clear picture of what's working and why.

⚠ The Test

Could you run this from your phone for a week? If not — it's not ready for Thailand. Fix it before you board the plane.
